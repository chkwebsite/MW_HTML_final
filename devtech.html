Technology for Development – Leveraging game changing solutions collecting field data, and providing decision support analysis – for better supporting the change that is attempted in improving the Quality of Life – working across the World, with the World, for the World.

In the social and development sector, including in rural areas, the interventions can be complex, and quality data collection a challenge. Microware specializes in providing solutions that address this issue through the use of on-ground collection of data both off-line and on-line, on mobile phones and tablets, on stand-alone PCs, as well as the web – end to end solutions for over 300 programs – from the local district level interventions to nation wide systems with thousands of users supporting millions of subjects.

In this domain – covering both in India and abroad – Government – National and regional, UN agencies, INGOs, local NGOs and bilateral support agencies – Microware has found one thread of commonality – applications evolve, and field conditions and user concerns mandate simple, effective solutions that do not impose additional burden on any of the stake-holders.

DevTech

Q:
How often do requirements change?
A:

Often, the end product is not fully understood, not visualised at the outset, and based on field realities and evolution of understanding over time, the clients’ fine-tune, and often enhance their requirement. Impractical modules or queries can also be removed, and surveyor / facilitator fatigue can also play a major role in the redesign of a system or survey. Included may be new processes, or more practical data collection systems, or even better analytics and data and information sharing – Prototypes, screens, test reports, maps, graphs, GPS – all help realise what additional features and inputs are required to build a more robust, scalable and secure solution, focused on providing substantially more practical outputs, supporting planning, operations and decision making.

Q:
How do we support this process ?
A:

With decades of experience, across team members working across thematic areas since 1996, we consult, advise, design, deploy and support – hearing the requirement, providing nudges where required, and creating a practical and workable process – one that is dynamic and can grow with time, yet fulfils the current needs as is best possible. This is piloted, fine tuned and evolves into a practical, comprehensive solution – maybe somewhat different from the initial system imagined – but one that is more relevant to the requirements on the ground. Microware has supported as much as 40-60% variations and change requests across a lot of programs – the effort is towards making a useful, workable system.

Q:
Does this process make it more expensive ?
A:

For Microware, Yes. For the client, generally, no, other than the fact that frequent changes can sometimes result in time overruns. This process is supported by us only because it is our investment in making the program a success, making our users happier, and this happiness translates into better data for better intervention support. Of course, the resultant references provide us an organic growth across segments, themes and organizations, which is beneficial in the long term.
